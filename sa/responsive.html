Responsive Display

This slider is responsive to screen width. Very small screens show just one slide at a time, small screens show 2 at a time, medium screens show 3 at a time, and large screens (e.g. full-screen on a desktop computer) show 4 slides at a time.

Third slide is a paragraph with a link to Google in it. Will the link take focus when it is not visible? Let's find out.
